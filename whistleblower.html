Types of Corruption We Handle

Bribery

Offering, giving, receiving, or soliciting money or gifts to influence official actions.

Embezzlement

Theft or misappropriation of public funds by officials entrusted with those assets.

Nepotism

Favoritism shown to family members or friends in hiring, promotion, or contract awards.

Abuse of Power

Using official position for personal gain or to harm others beyond lawful authority.

Fraud

Deliberate deception to secure unfair advantage or deprive others of legal rights.

Misuse of Funds

Using public money for unauthorized purposes or personal benefit.
