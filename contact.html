Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Nyaya Mitra and our services.

What is Nyaya Mitra?

Nyaya Mitra is an AI-powered legal technology platform designed to make legal services accessible to everyone in India. We provide tools for legal consultation, case tracking, document analysis, and emergency support.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes, most of our basic services are completely free. We believe in making legal assistance accessible to all citizens. Some premium features may have nominal charges to maintain the platform.

How secure is my data?

We take data security very seriously. All communications are encrypted, and we follow strict data protection protocols. For anonymous services like whistleblowing, we ensure complete anonymity.

Can I get real legal advice through the chatbot?

Our AI chatbot provides general legal information and guidance. While it's very knowledgeable, it cannot replace professional legal advice from a licensed lawyer for complex legal matters.

How do I report a technical issue?

You can report technical issues through our contact form above, send an email to support@nyayamitra.org, or use the AI chatbot for immediate assistance.

Is this project affiliated with any government agency?

Nyaya Mitra is an independent academic project developed by BCA students. While we work to complement government legal services, we are not officially affiliated with any government agency.
