How to Use Emergency SOS

Simple steps to ensure you get help when you need it most

01

Allow Location Access

Enable location services on your device for accurate positioning. This helps emergency responders find you quickly and provide timely assistance.

Your location is only shared during an active emergency
02

Press & Hold SOS Button

Press and hold the red emergency button for 3 seconds. The countdown timer will show your progress as you hold the button down.

Release before 3 seconds to cancel
03

Alert Automatically Sent

Your location, personal details, and emergency type are instantly shared with appropriate emergency services and your designated contacts.

Confirmation message appears when alert is sent
04

Receive Assistance

Emergency services will contact you and dispatch help to your location. Stay on the page if possible to receive status updates.

Be prepared to receive a call from responders

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our SOS alert system

Who receives my emergency alert?

Your emergency alert is simultaneously sent to:

  • The appropriate emergency service based on the type of emergency (police, ambulance, fire)
  • Your designated emergency contacts that you've added to your profile
  • The Nyaya Mitra support team who can provide additional assistance

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • Your location is only accessed when you activate the SOS alert
  • Personal and medical information is encrypted and stored securely
  • Data is only shared with emergency services during an active emergency
  • We comply with all applicable privacy laws and regulations

What happens if I accidentally trigger an alert?

The 3-second hold requirement helps prevent accidental triggers, but if you do accidentally send an alert:

  • You'll receive a confirmation message with a "Cancel Alert" option
  • You can immediately cancel the alert within 60 seconds of sending it
  • If emergency services contact you, simply explain it was an accidental trigger

What information is included in my emergency alert?

Your emergency alert includes:

  • Your current GPS location (latitude and longitude)
  • Your name and contact information
  • Any medical information you've added to your profile
  • The time the alert was triggered
  • Your emergency contacts' information

Can I use this service in areas with poor connectivity?

Yes, the system is designed to work even in areas with limited connectivity:

  • The app will attempt to send the alert using minimal data
  • If data connectivity is unavailable, it will attempt to send an SMS
  • The system will keep trying to send the alert until it succeeds
  • Your last known location will be included even if current GPS cannot be determined
