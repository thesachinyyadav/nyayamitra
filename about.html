Our Story

Nyaya Mitra was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: legal assistance should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background, education, or financial status.

The project emerged during the TechTank2024 hackathon where our team focused on addressing UN SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions. We witnessed firsthand how the digital divide and lack of legal awareness affected many communities. Legal processes in India can be complex and intimidating, leaving many citizens without the means to understand or exercise their rights.

With a shared passion for technology and social impact, our team set out to create an accessible platform that bridges this gap. Starting as an innovative idea at the hackathon, Nyaya Mitra evolved into our BCA mini project and has now become a comprehensive legal tech solution with a mission to democratize access to justice.

Our Mission & Values

The principles that drive our innovation and impact

Empower Citizens

Make legal services accessible to everyone through innovative technology and user-friendly platforms, ensuring that legal knowledge is no longer a privilege but a right for all citizens.

Our Approach: Simplified interfaces, multilingual support, and free access to basic legal information.

Ensure Justice

Promote transparency, accountability, and fair legal processes for all citizens of India, with special focus on underserved communities who face barriers to legal resources.

Our Approach: Whistleblower protection, rights awareness, and emergency legal support systems.

Leverage Technology

Utilize cutting-edge AI and machine learning to streamline legal processes and improve efficiency, making legal assistance more affordable, faster, and more accurate.

Our Approach: AI-powered legal chatbot, document analysis tools, and case tracking systems.

Build Community

Create a supportive community of legal professionals, citizens, and technology experts working together to improve legal accessibility and drive positive social change.

Our Approach: Open collaboration, knowledge sharing, and partnerships with legal organizations.
