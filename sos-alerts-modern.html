Safety Tips

Essential advice for emergency situations

Personal Safety

  • Stay aware of your surroundings at all times
  • Share your location with trusted friends when traveling alone
  • Keep emergency contacts easily accessible on your phone
  • Trust your instincts if a situation feels uncomfortable

Medical Emergencies

  • Learn basic first aid techniques
  • Create a list of medical conditions and allergies
  • Keep essential medications accessible
  • Know the closest hospital or emergency room to your location

Travel Safety

  • Ensure your vehicle has emergency supplies
  • Plan your route before traveling to unfamiliar areas
  • Keep your mobile phone charged while traveling
  • Avoid unsafe areas, especially at night
